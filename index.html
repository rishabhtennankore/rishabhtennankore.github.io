About me

I am a Materials Scientist with experience spanning polymer formulations, elastomer mechanics, and mass transport across membranes. Most recently, I worked on an early-stage technology startup R&D team developing scalable polymer and sol-gel nanoparticle assemblies for hydrophobic modification of porous substrates, enabling roll-to-roll–amenable pressure-driven distillation membranes. Outside the lab, I work as an independent technical consultant, partnering with applied R&D teams to bring clarity to materials selection, process development, and experimental decision-making. I’m also deeply interested in CAD-driven design—both for consulting work and personal DIY projects— and enjoy exploring hiking trails, motorcycles, woodworking and cooking as creative outlets.
